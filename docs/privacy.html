Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Booray (National Bourré League) is a social card-game app for private rooms, scorekeeping, and skill rankings. There are no payments, wallets, or money transfers.

Information we collect

  • Account data — email, display name, and profile photo when you sign up or use Google Sign-In.
  • Gameplay data — room membership, session scores, tricks won, Ape Score rankings, and in-room notes you enter.
  • Technical data — standard app and hosting logs needed to operate the service (e.g. errors, deploy version).

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • Run private rooms, live tables, and leaderboards.
  • Maintain fair play through server-side game validation.
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not process real-money gambling or payments.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising profiles.

Service providers

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Hosting) to run the app. Firebase processes data on our behalf under Google’s terms and privacy policy.

Data retention & deletion

Account and gameplay data are kept while your account is active. To request account deletion or data export, contact us via the Support page.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do.
